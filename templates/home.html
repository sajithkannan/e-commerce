E-commerce

E-commerce (electronic commerce) is the buying and selling of goods and services over the internet. It allows businesses and customers to trade products, make payments, and manage orders online without physical stores. E-commerce platforms can include online shops, marketplaces, and service booking sites like Amazon, Flipkart, or Myntra.
